Cruising Over Borders

Sep 6th, 2010

$3 billion is the value of export orders BrahMos Aerospace, a joint venture between the governments of India and Russia, has already placed for its new cruise missile, test-fired yesterday. There is “no plan to accept the conditions of the Missile Technology Control Regime.”

$18.8 million is how much Summit Builders Construction of Phoenix, Arizona, will get from the US Army Corps of Engineers to build a new, 450-man Border Patrol facility and a “major upgrade” to an existing facility in the town of Naco, on the Mexican border.
