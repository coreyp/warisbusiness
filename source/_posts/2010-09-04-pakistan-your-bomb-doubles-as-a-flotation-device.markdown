**[![](/images/2010/09/punjab-pakistan-flood-300x187.jpg)](http://multiamerican.scpr.org/2010/08/local-efforts-for-pakistan-flood-relief-step-up/)$217 million** is how much the US government is providing for "relief and recovery efforts"—including water treatment units, rescue boats and food—in Pakistan during the worst [floods](http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/gallery/2010/aug/13/pakistan-flooding) in that nation's history, [as of Sept. 4, 2010](http://www.state.gov/r/pa/prs/ps/2010/09/146825.htm).

**$2.1 billion** is the minimum aggregate value of the US government's military aid to Pakistan from 2001 through March of this year, for weapons—including nearly 6,000 missiles, 20 attack helicopters and 18 brand-new F-16 fighter jets ([pdf](http://www.google.com/url?sa=t&source=web&cd=1&ved=0CBUQFjAA&url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fas.org%2Fsgp%2Fcrs%2Frow%2Fpakarms.pdf&rct=j&q=%22Major%20U.S.%20Arms%20Sales%20and%20Grants%20to%20Pakistan%20Since%202001%22&ei=a9GCTOKdB4z6sAOs9az3Bw&usg=AFQjCNH_F09jt1YM0MQt8eGYd4wOxqlthw&sig2=sM7Bgg7mEwEjfk94waXWPg)).
